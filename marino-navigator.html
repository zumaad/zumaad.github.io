Marino-Navigator

A tool that helps newcomers at my university gym locate equipment

Video Demo

https://youtu.be/8TdfYuAmlTU

How does it help newcomers?

  • Users can search for specific equipment if they know the name
  • Users can click on a bodypart and get all the equipment that works it out
  • When they click on an equipment name or look it up, they get to see all instances of that equipment, all their various locations, and a floorplan that reflects their location

Marino-Navigator

A tool that helps newcomers at my university gym locate equipment

Video Demo

https://youtu.be/8TdfYuAmlTU

How does it help newcomers?

  • Users can search for specific equipment if they know the name
  • Users can click on a bodypart and get all the equipment that works it out
  • When they click on an equipment name or look it up, they get to see all instances of that equipment, all their various locations, and a floorplan that reflects their location

How is the data structured/modeled?

To start, here is a picture of the database schema

There are 5 tables: equipment contains the name and description of a peice of equipment. location contains a floor and room number. body_parts_targeted contains the name of bodyparts that you can workout, like "chest","calves",etc. The rest of the tables contain foreign keys, defining relationships between the other tables. location_equipment contains references to individual locations and their corresponding equipment. This allows me to query a table and find out where "bench press" is located, for example. equipment_body_parts contains references to equipment and bodyparts so I can query it and find out all the equipment that works out a certain muscle group.
